### How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Kim-Abo account and all associated data from our cloud services, you can do so directly from the app:

1. **Open Kim-Abo app**
2. **Go to Settings** (third tab)
3. **Scroll to "Cloud Sync" section** (Premium feature)
4. **If you are signed in**, click **"Delete Account"** button
5. **Confirm deletion** in the dialog that appears

### What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our cloud services:

‚úÖ **All subscription data** (names, amounts, dates, categories)  
‚úÖ **Price history** for all subscriptions  
‚úÖ **User account information** (email, user ID)  
‚úÖ **Synchronization timestamps** and metadata  
‚úÖ **All data stored in Firebase Firestore**

### What Is NOT Deleted

‚ùó **Local data on your device** remains intact:

- All local data continues to work normally

### Technical Implementation

Our deletion process:
- **Immediate**: All cloud data is deleted from Firebase Firestore within minutes
- **Complete**: Includes all subcollections and related documents
- **Irreversible**: Deleted data cannot be recovered
- **Automatic sign-out**: User is logged out after successful deletion
